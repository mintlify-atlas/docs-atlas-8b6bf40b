Neo Letter is ready to play the moment you open it. Your browser receives a guest session automatically, so you can create a room, invite friends, and start guessing five-letter words without signing up for anything. If you want your wins and points to persist across devices, you can link a Google account at any time.

## Player flow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the site">
    Open [neo-letter.web.app](https://neo-letter.web.app) in your browser.

    On your first visit, Neo Letter assigns you a guest identity backed by a UUID stored in a cookie (`neo-letter-game-uid`). No account or email is required. The cookie persists for one year, so your guest session survives page refreshes and browser restarts.

    <Note>
      Neo Letter is a Progressive Web App (PWA). On mobile, tap **Add to Home Screen** to install it and play offline between sessions.
    </Note>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create or join a room">
    From the home page, choose one of two paths:

    - **Create** — opens the room configuration screen where you set the player mode, number of rounds, privacy, and optional custom words. Once configured, click **Create Party** to generate a new room with a five-digit numeric code (e.g. `04821`).
    - **Join** — enter a five-digit room code shared by another player to jump straight into their lobby.

    Room modes available when creating:

    | Mode | Max players |
    | --- | --- |
    | Solo | 1 |
    | Versus | 2 |
    | Party | 5 |
    | Mega Party | 10 |
    | Custom | Any number you set |
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get ready in the lobby">
    After entering a room, you land in the lobby. Mark yourself as **Ready** to signal that you're set to play. The game starts as soon as every player in the room has toggled ready.

    While waiting, you can use the in-room chat (if the room creator left it enabled) to coordinate with other players.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Guess the word">
    Each round, every player receives the same hidden five-letter word. Type your guess and press **Enter**. Tiles change color after each submission:

    - **Green** — correct letter, correct position
    - **Yellow** — correct letter, wrong position
    - **Gray** — letter not in the word

    The first player to guess correctly wins the round and earns points. Fewer guesses used means more points. After all rounds are complete, the player with the highest cumulative score wins the game.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Sign in to track your stats

Playing as a guest is fully supported, but signing in with Google unlocks stat tracking. Your profile records:

- **Wins** — number of multiplayer games where you finished first place
- **Games played** — number of completed game sessions
- **Points** — cumulative score earned

To sign in, click the account icon in the header and choose **Sign in with Google**. Your guest session seamlessly upgrades — the UUID cookie is replaced with your Google account UID so you never lose your place in an active room.
